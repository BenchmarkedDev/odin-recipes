Chicken Curry

Rice with peas and sweetcorn topped with tender chicken chunks and delicious curry sauce

Master my all-time favourite dish with this fantastic Chicken Curry recipe!

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken Breast
  • 150g Peas
  • 150g Sweetcorn
  • Your favourite Curry Sauce

Steps

  1. Simultaneously cook the chicken breast on medium heat, and the rice on low heat
  2. Mix sweetcorn and peas together in a bowl
  3. When the colour of the chicken starts to come to life, add in your curry sauce and stir often
  4. Once rice has finished cooking, add to plate(s)
  5. Pour the curry over the center of the rice
