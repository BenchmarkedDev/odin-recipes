Pizza

An all-time favourite dish, beloved by all humans.

Experiment with toppings to find the perfect pizza for you and your loved ones!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flower
  • Yeast
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes/Tomato Puree
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Break eggs into a jug
  2. Add eggs into a mix of yeast and flour
  3. Use your hands to mix the ingredients until doughy
  4. Use your hands once again to form the dough into a pizza-base shape
  5. Make your own tomato sauce or use puree to spread across the base
  6. Grate your cheese across the entire pizza. Don't miss an inch!
  7. Scatter your chosen toppings across the pizza
  8. Pop your creation into the oven on 180 for approx 20 minutes, or until slightly browned.
