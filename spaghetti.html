Spaghetti Bolognese

Tender beef and soft spaghetti in a delicious tomato sauce

Learn to master your inner italian chef with this wonderful Spaghetti Bolognese recipe! Make mouths water by learning to how to craft the absolute best pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Minced Beef
  • 500g Spaghetti
  • 200g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 150g Grated Cheddar
  • 50g Diced Onion
  • Sprinkle of Garlic & Herbs

Steps

  1. Cook the minced beef on medium heat until crisp and brown
  2. Place the spaghetti in boiling water
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to a bowl
  4. Dice onion and add to the same bowl
  5. Mix tomatoes and onions together and add to minced beef, stir thoroughly
  6. Once cooked, empty spaghetti onto plate(s)
  7. Place a generous helping of the meat, tomato and onion mix in the center of the spaghetti
  8. Scatter grated cheese on top of the meat
  9. Sprinkle garlic and herbs over the entire dish
